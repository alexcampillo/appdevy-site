    • The Challenge

    With Southampton seeing a surge in trendy, upmarket food vendors, Grazing Goat in Southampton offers a modern take on the traditional family pub, serving quality, well sourced food. Hatch was tasked with creating a brand that married those two ideas.

    The Solution

    We created something illustrative of the name, iconic and instantly classic for the new venue. Careful typography choices were made to support the bespoke illustration of a goat, well.. grazing! The result was used on menus, signage, staff uniforms and even a hand carved wooden storage box.
