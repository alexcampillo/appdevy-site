    • The Challenge

    To turn Southampton's historical Northam Road into a 21st Century Antique's hub. The site had to fit in with the artwork provided by the local community, be fully responsive and logically organise various types of information.

    The Solution

    We worked with Southampton City Council to understand the look and feel of the re-marketing of Northam Road and built a mobile first, completely bespoke Wordpress theme which matched their existing brand assets and type choices perfectly.
