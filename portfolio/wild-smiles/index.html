    • The Challenge

    Southampton based Wild Smiles had quite an idea for a website to promote their forthcoming single ‘Fool For You’. The challenge was to create a digital but classic looking vinyl player complete with animation and audio support.

    The Solution

    Working with Rob Da Bank’s Sunday Best, we put together a fully interactive vinyl player to showcase the new single. The result puts the music front and centre and gives fans a bit more than just a Tumblr blog.
